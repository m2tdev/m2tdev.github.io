Ord. Prof. Dr. Ata Nutku

The man who built the first ship in Turkey

Chronology of Ata Nutku's Life

  • 1904 - Ata Nutku born at Preveza.
  • 1923 - Finished Naval Academy.
  • 1928 - He went to Italy for acceptance of the first "Tepe" class torpedo boats at Ansaldo Shipyard while he was major.
  • 1931-1938 - He built the first ship in the Republic of Turkey. The ship called "Gölcük Yağ Gemisi" which weighs 1250 tons at the Gölcük Shipyard with a lot of suffering between these years.
  • 1939 - He was sent to UK for shipbuilding higher engineering education.
  • 1943 - He lead the foundation of "Ship Building Higher Engineering" department at Istanbul Technical University(ITU).
  • 1944 - He taught at ITU and achieved the professor title.
  • 1941-42 - He modernized the Gölcük and the Taşkızak shipyards.
  • 1948 - He retired from marine army while he was lieutenant colonel.
  • 1953 - He found the Ship Institute of Turkey.
  • 1960 - He became the father of shipbuilding in Turkey and Ordinarius Professor.
  • 1958-59 - He taught at California University.
  • 1969-70 - He taught at Maryland University
  • 1974 - He retired from ITU. Same year TUBITAK honored him with an award because of his awesome labors.
  • He wrote two books about Ship Design (1958) and Reciprocating Steam Machines (1968).
  • 1994 - Sadly he passed away.
